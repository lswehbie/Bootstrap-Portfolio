About Me

Graduated from North Carolina State University with a B.S. Design Studies and a minor in Art and Design in 2015. I am a producer under the name "Blursome." I've been creating music for the past eight years. I focus on sound manipulation through found sound from field recordings and sampling. I mainly use the music software Ableton. I am a Red Bull Music Academy - Bass Camp alum. Im with the record label Hotflush Recordings and am currently working on my second release with the label.
